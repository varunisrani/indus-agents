Understanding Your Results

Normal Levels

Blood oxygen levels between 95-100% are considered normal for healthy individuals. Levels below 95% may indicate hypoxemia and should be discussed with a healthcare provider.

What Affects SpO2

Various factors can affect blood oxygen levels including altitude, lung conditions, heart conditions, sleep apnea, anemia, and certain medications. Track trends over time rather than focusing on single readings.
