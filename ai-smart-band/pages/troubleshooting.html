Troubleshooting

Solutions to common issues

Band not connecting to phone

  1. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled
  2. Restart both devices
  3. Unpair and repair the band
  4. Update the app to latest version

Battery draining quickly

  1. Turn off always-on display
  2. Reduce heart rate monitoring frequency
  3. Disable unnecessary notifications
  4. Check for software updates

Inaccurate step counting

  1. Wear the band correctly on your wrist
  2. Calibrate stride length in app settings
  3. Ensure firmware is up to date
