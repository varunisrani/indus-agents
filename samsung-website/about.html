About Samsung

We're guided by a simple belief: everything we do should inspire possibility. Do What You Can't.

Our Mission

To inspire the world with our innovative technologies, products, and design that enrich people's lives and contribute to social prosperity by creating a new future.

Our History

Decades of innovation and growth

1969

Samsung Founded

Samsung-Sanyong Electronics was established in Suwon, Korea, marking the beginning of our journey.

1988

Entering the Mobile Era

Launched our first mobile phone, beginning our leadership in mobile communications.

1999

Digital Age Leadership

Became a global leader in digital technology, semiconductors, and displays.

2010

Galaxy Revolution

Launched the first Galaxy S smartphone, transforming the mobile industry.

2020

AI & Future Tech

Accelerated investment in AI, 5G, and cutting-edge semiconductor technologies.

2024

Galaxy AI Era

Introduced Galaxy AI, bringing intelligent experiences to millions of users worldwide.
