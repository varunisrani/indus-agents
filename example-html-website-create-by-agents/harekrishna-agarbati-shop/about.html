Our Story

In the sacred land of Vrindavan, where Lord Krishna spent his childhood, HareKrishna Agarbati Shop was born from a deep devotion to spreading divine fragrances across the world. Founded in 1985 by Pandit Radhe Shyam Ji, our family business has been serving spiritual seekers for over three decades.

What started as a small workshop crafting traditional incense for local temples has grown into a beloved brand trusted by thousands of devotees worldwide. Yet, our commitment to authenticity, quality, and spiritual purity remains unchanged.

Every agarbati we create is infused with prayers and made using ancient recipes passed down through generations. We believe that fragrance is not just a sensory experience but a pathway to the divine, helping elevate consciousness and create sacred spaces in homes and temples.

Our Craftsmanship

The art of creating divine fragrances

Ancient Techniques, Modern Quality

Our artisans begin each day with prayers, infusing positive energy into every agarbati they create. The process starts with carefully selecting the finest natural ingredients - sandalwood from Mysore, roses from Kannauj, jasmine from Madurai, and sacred herbs from the Himalayas.

These ingredients are dried, ground, and blended according to secret family recipes that have been perfected over generations. The masala (paste) is then hand-rolled onto bamboo sticks using traditional techniques that ensure even burning and consistent fragrance release.

Each stick is carefully dried in natural sunlight, allowing the fragrances to mature and develop their full aromatic potential. The entire process can take up to 7 days, ensuring that every agarbati meets our exacting standards.
