Common questions

How do I set up my new iPhone?

Turn on your iPhone by pressing and holding the side button until you see the Apple logo. Then follow the setup assistant to:

  • Choose your language and region
  • Connect to Wi-Fi
  • Transfer data from your old device
  • Set up Face ID or Touch ID
  • Create or sign in with your Apple ID
What should I do if my iPhone won't charge?

Try these steps:

  1. Check your charging cable and adapter for damage
  2. Clean your iPhone's charging port
  3. Try a different charging cable and adapter
  4. Restart your iPhone
  5. If the issue persists, contact Apple Support
How can I extend my iPhone's battery life?

To improve battery life:

  • Enable Low Power Mode in Settings > Battery
  • Reduce screen brightness or enable Auto-Brightness
  • Turn off Location Services for apps you don't use
  • Disable background app refresh for unused apps
  • Keep your iPhone updated with the latest iOS
What should I do if my iPhone gets wet?

If your iPhone gets wet:

  1. Turn off the device immediately
  2. Remove any accessories and SIM card
  3. Wipe with a lint-free cloth
  4. Leave to dry in a well-ventilated area
  5. Do not use heat or compressed air
  6. Wait at least 48 hours before turning back on
