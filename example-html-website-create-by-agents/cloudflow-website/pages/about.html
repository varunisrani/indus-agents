About CloudFlow

We're on a mission to transform how businesses work by making automation accessible to everyone.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2019, CloudFlow started with a simple idea: make workflow automation accessible to everyone, not just developers. Our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of repetitive manual tasks and knew there had to be a better way.

Today, we serve over 10,000 companies worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our platform has automated millions of workflows, saving teams countless hours and enabling them to focus on what truly matters.

We're just getting started. Our vision is to become the operating system for business operations, powering the next generation of automated workflows.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Our core values guide everything we do, from product decisions to how we treat our customers.

Customer First

Our customers' success is our success. We obsess over their needs and go above and beyond to deliver exceptional experiences.

Continuous Innovation

We never stop improving. We constantly iterate, experiment, and push boundaries to deliver the best possible solution.

Transparency

We believe in open communication and honesty. We share our progress, challenges, and decisions openly with our team.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do. From code quality to customer support, we accept nothing less than the best.

Integrity

We do the right thing, even when no one is watching. We build trust through consistent, ethical behavior.

Learning

We're lifelong learners. We embrace curiosity, seek feedback, and grow from our mistakes.
