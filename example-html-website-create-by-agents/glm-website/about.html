Our Mission

At GLM, we believe that AI should be a force for good in the world. Our mission is to build safe, reliable, and beneficial AI systems that augment human capabilities and help solve humanity's greatest challenges. We're committed to developing AI that is transparent, accountable, and aligned with human values.

Our Journey

Key milestones in GLM's development

2023

Foundation

GLM was founded by a team of AI researchers and engineers with a shared vision for safe, beneficial AI.

2024

First Model Release

Launched GLM-Base, our first production-ready language model, setting new benchmarks for safety and performance.

2024

Pro & Ultra Launch

Released GLM-Pro and GLM-Ultra, bringing advanced reasoning capabilities to developers worldwide.

Global Expansion

Expanded to serve millions of users across 150+ countries, with multi-language support and regional deployments.
