Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about pricing

What is a token?

Tokens are pieces of words used for processing. Roughly, 1,000 tokens equals about 750 words. You're charged based on the number of tokens in both your input and the model's output.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, you'll receive credit towards future billing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers. For annual plans, we also offer invoicing.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes! New users get a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. After the trial, you can continue with the Free plan or upgrade to Pro.

Do you offer discounts for startups or education?

Yes! We offer special pricing for startups and educational institutions. Contact our sales team to learn more about our startup and education programs.

What's included in Enterprise support?

Enterprise support includes 24/7 phone support, dedicated account manager, custom integrations, SLA guarantees, and priority access to new features and improvements.
