Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Registering your Samsung product is easy. Visit our product registration page and enter your device's model number and serial number. You can also use the Samsung Members app on your Galaxy device to register automatically. Registration helps you receive software updates, warranty service, and exclusive offers.
Samsung products come with a standard one-year limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. The warranty does not cover damage from accidents, misuse, unauthorized repairs, or cosmetic damage. Extended warranty options are available for purchase for additional coverage.
To check for software updates on your Samsung device, go to Settings > Software Update. Tap "Download and install" to check for available updates. For Galaxy devices, you can also enable automatic updates to receive the latest features and security patches as soon as they're available.
The model number can usually be found in several places: on the original packaging, on a sticker inside the battery compartment (for devices with removable batteries), or in Settings > About phone > Status (for mobile devices). For appliances, check the inside of the door or the back of the unit.
Yes, Samsung offers extended warranty plans called Samsung Care+ that provide additional coverage beyond the standard warranty. These plans can be purchased within 60 days of buying your product or when your standard warranty is still active. Visit the Samsung Care section for more information and to enroll.
You can contact Samsung support through multiple channels: call us at 1-800-SAMSUNG (726-7864), use the live chat on our support website, send a message through the Samsung Members app, or visit our community forums for peer-to-peer support. Our support team is available Monday through Saturday.
