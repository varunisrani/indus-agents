Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I integrate NexusAI into my app?

Most developers can integrate our SDKs within a few hours. We provide comprehensive documentation, sample code, and quick-start guides to get you up and running quickly. Complex implementations may take 1-2 weeks depending on your requirements.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose a plan that fits your needs or continue with our free tier for small projects.

Is my data secure with NexusAI?

Absolutely. We're GDPR and CCPA compliant. All AI processing can be done on-device, meaning your user data never leaves the phone. For cloud-based features, we use enterprise-grade encryption and security measures.

What platforms do you support?

We support iOS (Swift/Objective-C), Android (Kotlin/Java), React Native, Flutter, Xamarin, and web applications. Our SDKs are designed to work seamlessly across all major mobile and web platforms.

Can I train custom AI models?

Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans include custom model training. You can train models with your own data, and we'll help optimize them for mobile deployment. Contact our sales team for more information.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer multiple support tiers: Community support for all users, email support for Professional plans, and 24/7 priority support with dedicated account managers for Enterprise customers. We also have extensive documentation and tutorials available.
