At AI Smart Band, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Health Data: Heart rate, sleep patterns, steps, calories, SpO2, stress levels, and other health metrics collected by your device.

Account Information: Name, email, password, and profile information you provide when creating an account.

Usage Data: How you use our app and features, device information, and crash reports.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Track your fitness and health progress over time
  • Improve our products and services
  • Communicate with you about updates and offers
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • HIPAA and GDPR compliance

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your data

Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf
  • Healthcare providers with your explicit consent
  • Legal authorities when required by law

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at: privacy@aismartband.com
