Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1: Charge Your Band

Before first use, charge your AI Smart Band fully using the provided magnetic charging cable. This takes approximately 2 hours.

Step 2: Download the App

Download the AI Smart Band app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Create your account or sign in.

Step 3: Pair Your Device

Enable Bluetooth on your phone. Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your AI Smart Band. Hold the band close to your phone.

Step 4: Complete Setup

Enter your personal information including age, height, weight, and fitness goals. This helps provide accurate tracking and personalized insights.
