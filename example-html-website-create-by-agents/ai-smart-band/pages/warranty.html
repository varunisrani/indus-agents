Warranty Information

Your coverage and protection details

1-Year Limited Warranty

AI Smart Band comes with a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions under normal use.

✅ Covered

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Hardware malfunctions
  • Battery failure
  • Sensor defects
  • Button failures
  • Charging port issues

❌ Not Covered

  • Physical damage (drops, cracks)
  • Water damage from misuse
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Lost or stolen devices
  • Unauthorized modifications
  • Software issues (free updates available)
