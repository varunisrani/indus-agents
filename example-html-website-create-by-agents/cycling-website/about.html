About CyclePro

Passionate cyclists dedicated to sharing the joy of riding with our community

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by cycling enthusiasts, CyclePro started as a small repair shop in a garage. Our passion for two wheels and dedication to the cycling community has helped us grow into one of the region's most trusted cycling destinations.

We believe that cycling is more than just a mode of transportation – it's a lifestyle, a community, and a path to better health and environmental sustainability. Every bike we sell, every repair we make, and every customer we serve is a testament to our commitment to this vision.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of cyclists, from beginners to professional athletes, offering premium products, expert services, and a welcoming community for all.
