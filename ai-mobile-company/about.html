About NexusAI

Our Story

From a small startup to a global AI leader

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate AI researchers and mobile technology experts, NexusAI began with a simple mission: to make artificial intelligence accessible, practical, and powerful on mobile devices.

We recognized that the future of computing was mobile, and that AI had the potential to transform how we interact with our devices. Our founders, having worked at leading tech companies and research institutions, brought together decades of expertise in machine learning, mobile development, and user experience design.

Today, NexusAI powers over 10 million mobile devices worldwide, our AI processes billions of requests daily, and we've partnered with Fortune 500 companies to deliver next-generation mobile experiences. But we're just getting started.
