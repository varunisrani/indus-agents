FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about CloudFlow.

How quickly can I get started with CloudFlow?

+

You can start using CloudFlow immediately after signing up. Our onboarding process takes less than 5 minutes, and you can create your first workflow in under 10 minutes. We also offer guided tutorials and 24/7 support to help you get started.

Do I need coding skills to use CloudFlow?

+

No coding skills required! CloudFlow is designed to be used by anyone, regardless of technical expertise. Our visual drag-and-drop builder makes it easy to create complex workflows without writing a single line of code.

What integrations do you support?

+

CloudFlow integrates with over 500 popular apps and services, including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and many more. We're constantly adding new integrations based on customer feedback.

Is my data secure with CloudFlow?

+

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and use enterprise-grade encryption for all data. We also offer advanced security features like SSO, role-based access control, and audit logs for enterprise customers.
