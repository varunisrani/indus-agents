About Sandalwood Sacred

Our Sandalwood Sacred Agarbati represents the pinnacle of traditional Indian incense craftsmanship. Made from the finest quality Mysore sandalwood (Santalum album), each stick carries the sacred essence that has been revered in Ayurveda and spiritual practices for thousands of years.

The sandalwood used in our agarbati is sourced from sustainable forests in Karnataka, where the trees are allowed to mature naturally for at least 30 years before harvest. This ensures the highest concentration of natural oils and the most authentic fragrance.

Perfect for daily puja, meditation sessions, or simply creating a peaceful atmosphere in your home. The calming aroma helps reduce stress, improve focus, and promote spiritual well-being.

100% Natural Ingredients

  • Pure Mysore Sandalwood (Santalum album) - 60%
  • Natural Himalayan Herbs - 20%
  • Sacred Temple Flowers - 10%
  • Natural Honey (as binder) - 5%
  • Essential Oils - 5%

No synthetic fragrances, chemicals, or artificial ingredients. 100% natural and safe for regular use.

Origin & Craftsmanship

Source: Our sandalwood is ethically sourced from sustainable forests in Karnataka, India. We work directly with local farmers who follow traditional harvesting methods.

Craftsmanship: Each agarbati is hand-rolled by skilled artisans in Vrindavan using techniques passed down through generations. The entire process, from preparation to packaging, takes 7 days.

Blessings: Before packaging, each batch is blessed at the Banke Bihari Temple in Vrindavan, infusing it with divine energy.
