Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does the IPO process typically take?

+

The typical IPO process takes 6-12 months from initial preparation to listing. However, this timeline can vary based on your company's readiness, market conditions, and regulatory requirements. We work efficiently while ensuring thorough preparation.

What makes AI IPOs different from traditional IPOs?

+

AI companies often have unique business models, intellectual property considerations, and growth metrics that require specialized understanding. Our team's deep AI expertise allows us to effectively communicate your technology and market opportunity to investors.

How do you determine the optimal IPO pricing?

+

We use a combination of financial analysis, comparable company analysis, market conditions, and investor feedback to determine optimal pricing. Our goal is to maximize your valuation while ensuring a successful first-day trading performance.

What ongoing support do you provide after the IPO?

+

We provide comprehensive post-IPO support including investor relations, earnings preparation, continuous compliance, strategic advisory, and planning for secondary offerings or M&A activities. Our partnership extends well beyond the IPO date.
